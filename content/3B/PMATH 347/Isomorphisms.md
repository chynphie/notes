> [!definition] Bijection
> Let $f:X\to Y$ be a function, then $f$ is:
> 	1. **injective** if for all $x,y\in X,\:f(x)=f(y)\implies x=y$
> 	2. **surjective** if for all $y\in Y,\:\exists x\in X$ with $f(x)=y$
> 	3. **bijective** if both injective and surjective

> [!proposition] 
> - $f:X\to Y$ is bijec. **iff** there's a function $g:Y\to X$ s.t. $f◦ g = 1_Y\:and\: g ◦ f = 1_{X}$ 

> [!definition] Isomorphisms
> $\phi:G\to H$ is an isomorphism if $\phi$ is a **bijective Homomorphism**

> [!lemma] 
> $\phi:G\to H$ is an isomo. **iff** ker $\phi=\{ e_{G} \}$ and $lm\phi=H$
